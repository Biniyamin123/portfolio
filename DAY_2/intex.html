When we discuss this element, it's all about mindset. We can have two types of mindsets: a fixed mindset or a growth mindset. With a fixed mindset, we believe that we can't change or improve, and the skills we have now are the only ones we'll ever have. A growth mindset, on the other hand, invites challenges. It welcomes the opportunity to learn new skills, grow, and improve ourselves. A growth mindset is key when it comes to motivation.
